# MOSAIC

Mount the following layouts (mobile first)
### 1. **Mobile version: all squares inline (column), with scroll**

```sh
 1 (fixed optional)
-----
2 | 3
-----
4 | 5
-----
6 | 7
----
8 | 9
```

### 2. **Tablet version**
```sh
1  |  2 | 3
--------
4 | 5 | 6
--------
7 | 8 | 9
```

### 3. **Desktop version**
```sh
1
----------- 
2  |  3  | 4
-----------
5 | 6 | 7 | 8
-----------
9 (optional fixed)
```
